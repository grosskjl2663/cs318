Bidoof

Bidoof (Japanese: ビッパ Bippa) is a non-player character in Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time, Darkness, and Sky. He is a member of Wigglytuff's Guild. He stars in his own Special Episode in Explorers of Sky, entitled Bidoof's Wish. He is also a recruitable Pokémon in Pokémon Super Mystery Dungeon.

Charizard

Charizard (Japanese: リザードン Lizardon) is a playable character in the Super Smash Bros. series. He originally appeared in the series when being summoned from a Poké Ball, and later became a playable character in Brawl, first as a part of the Pokémon Trainer's party, and later as an independent character in subsequent installments.

Piplup

Piplup (Japanese: ポッチャマ Pochama) is a major character in PokéPark Wii: Pikachu's Adventure and PokéPark 2: Wonders Beyond. He is Pikachu's best friend.

Wigglytuff

Wigglytuff (Japanese: プクリン Pukurin) is a non-player character in Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time and Explorers of Darkness and Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Sky. He is the Guildmaster of Wigglytuff's Guild and also a former member of the legendary Master Rank rescue team Team Charm. His assistant is Chatot.

Virizion

Virizion is one of the major characters in Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Gates to Infinity.

Pikachu

Pikachu (Japanese: ピカチュウ Pikachu) is the main playable character in PokéPark Wii: Pikachu's Adventure and PokéPark 2: Wonders Beyond.
